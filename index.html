About Me

I am currently pursuing my PhD at University Malaysia Pahang (UMP) in Machine Learning. Assistant professor in the CS department of AIUB on leave. I have real-life experience of software development, project management and currently indulged into teaching. Completed my Master and Bachelor degree from AIUB, Higher Secondary from Rajuk Uttara Model College and Secondary schooling from Ideal School and College. Besides my routine works, I love to serve open-source projects. I have a keen interest in technological advancement and new gadgets. My research interest primarily focuses on Data and Text Mining, Machine Learning and Knowledge Management.
